About

Muhammad Kutty Panaparambil Ismail (IPA: [muhɐmːɐd̪ kuʈːi pɐnɐpːɐrɐmbil ismɐjl]; born 7 September 1951), known mononymously by the hypocorism Mammootty (IPA: [mɐmːuːʈːi]), is an Indian actor and film producer who works predominantly in Malayalam films. He has also appeared in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Hindi, and English-language productions. In a career spanning five decades, he has acted in over 400 films. He is the recipient of several accolades, including three National Film Awards, seven Kerala State Film Awards, and thirteen Filmfare Awards South. For his contribution to film, the Government of India awarded him the Padma Shri in 1998. In 2022, he was honoured with Kerala Prabha Award, the second-highest honour given by the Government of Kerala
